About

AzFilesOptimizer helps customers and field teams quickly assess Azure Files environments and identify high-confidence Azure NetApp Files (ANF) modernization opportunities.

This Azure-native solution is deployed into your own subscription, using Azure PaaS services to provide discovery, analysis, and AI-powered recommendations.
